Ratatouille

Description

It is a vegan dish made of eggplant, zucchini, and tomatoes in a sauce of crushed tomatoes, herbs, garlic, onion, and carrots

It is also from the hit Pixar movie by the same name

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Watch the hit film Ratatouille
  2. Realise that "rat" is in it
  3. Recognize that you will not be able to make the dish without a rat
  4. Find your local rat to complete the dish
